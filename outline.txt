Color Palette
Soft Pink (#FFC0CB)
Pastel Blue (#AEC6CF)
Lavender (#E6E6FA)
Gold (#FFD700)
Cream or Off-White (#FFF8E7)
Deep Purple (#9370DB)
Dusky Rose (#DAA520)

Where to Use the Colors
Backgrounds: Soft Pink, Pastel Blue, and Cream can be used for backgrounds of different sections or pages. 
Lavender can be used for text and content containers.

Text and Typography: Lavender or Cream can be used for text to ensure readability. 
Deep Purple can be used for headings or accents.

Buttons and Interactive Elements: Gold, Pastel Blue, or Soft Pink can be used for buttons to make them eye-catching and interactive.

Decorative Elements: Deep Purple and Dusky Rose can be used for decorative elements, borders, 
or illustrations that add a touch of romance and creativity.

Icons: Gold can be used for icons or small decorative elements to give them a luxurious feel.

Navigation: You can use Soft Pink, Lavender, 
or Gold for navigation elements like buttons or links to make them easily accessible and visually appealing.

Fonts:
